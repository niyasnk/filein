Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this portal?

This application is your own private portal to safely and securely send information and files to niyas k. It’s far more secure than email, and helps by clearly outlining the requests for information, manages the communication and reminders, and simplifies your job by clearly providing an easy to use upload area. Once you have sent the files, they will be locked and secure.

2. How does it work?

niyas k, needs some information from you so they have setup this portal. niyas needs the information by 29/06/2023. It’s now your job to upload all the information before 29/06/2023. Don’t worry if you’re busy we all forget sometimes, so you will receive the odd email to remind you on, or around the due date. In order to complete everything, simply upload any files requested, or fill in any information niyas has asked for. Don’t worry if you don't have everything all at once, you can come back later and anything you upload will be saved. If you do have everything on hand now, just upload and save it all then click the I’m Finished button, and you’re done. It’s that simple! Once you have finished, all the information will be sent to niyas k, and they can review and check off the information. If anything needs to be changed, you will be notified and can come back and upload new information. The contact details for niyas k are under the contact tab on the left, and if you have any questions throughout the process you can click on the questions link on the left, and your query will go straight to niyas to answer it for you.

3. How do I upload?

To upload a file, click on each individual request listed under the requests page. This will open up showing more information about each request, and show an upload button. Click on the upload button then select the file(s) you wish to send to niyas k. When you have finished uploading, you can either click I’m Finished, or come back later to pick up where you left off.
